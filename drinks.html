Sprite

Sprite is a popular lemon-lime flavored carbonated soft drink produced by The Coca-Cola Company. It was introduced in 1961 as a competitor to 7 Up. Sprite is known for its clear, caffeine-free formula and refreshing taste. The beverage is widely available in various countries and is recognized for its crisp and citrusy flavor profile

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage that originated in Italy. It is made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, resulting in a small but highly concentrated shot of coffee. The process typically involves using an espresso machine, which pressurizes the water and passes it through the coffee grounds at a specific temperature and pressure.

Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is also a popular beverage made from cocoa powder, sugar, and milk. It is often served hot and may include additional ingredients like whipped cream or marshmallows. Hot chocolate is enjoyed as a comforting and indulgent drink, especially during colder seasons.
